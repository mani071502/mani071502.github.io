I am a curly hair specialist and i work with all types of curls. Many people refer to me as the curl doctor.I help to hydrate curls and people seem to be confused by the fact that curly hair cannot be brushed, maybe if I showed them they would understand.

 

Believe it or not, shampoo is really not necessary and is actually pretty bad for your curls! Try a cleansing conditioner, sulfate-free shampoo, or just a good old-fashioned scrub to keep your hair clean and healthy.Dry air sucks out all of the moisture, hat hair is a serious issue, and unless your hair is completely dry, there’s a good chance it will freeze. Let’s face it, winter is not our friend.
